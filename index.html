Vision + Mission

HopeXchange is a Christian 501(c) organization created to provide resource, mentorship and coaching for local high impact initiatives across Africa. Our mission is to promote local solutions to meet most urgent needs in Africa, through partneships with international community of Faith.

Programs - Mission In Action

Servant Leader Training (SLT)

Under our Servant Leadership training, young adults embark on a life-changing journey of deep self-discovery, character formation, spiritual growth, and learning how to lead through servanthood for 9 months.

View details »

Young Leaders Forum (YLF)

A young leaders club within the schools, which offers in-depth, experience-based servant leadership training that builds up young people and equips them for the future to be able to bring positive change in their communities.

View details »

Youth Disciple Program

A weekly character development program. Here we create an environment where young people, who are eager to connect with other youth in an atmosphere of fun, receive mentorship in godly character, and self-awareness.
